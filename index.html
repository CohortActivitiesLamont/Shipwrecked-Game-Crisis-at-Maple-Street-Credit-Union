Opening Line

"Notice which teams rebuilt fastest. What did they have in common? They didn't default to the tools they already knew — they picked tools based on what the problem actually needed. That's exactly the mindset we need when we talk about stablecoins today."

Key Reveal Points

⚡ Teams who picked the Stablecoin Rail early could settle payments instantly without the broken core system.

🔪 Smart Contracts automated payroll — no manual processing needed during the crisis.

🗺️ Compliance License was scarce — teams that missed it couldn't legally operate new tools.
